About EVWA

Built for Ethiopia.
Competitive with the world.

EVWA is modeled after globally recognized platforms like DVWA and HackTheBox, but localized for Ethiopia — with Amharic language support, culturally relevant challenge scenarios, and content designed for the Ethiopian cybersecurity ecosystem.

Students, developers, and security researchers can practice real-world attack techniques — including the full OWASP Top 10 — without harming real systems. Every challenge runs in an isolated Docker container, ensuring a safe and repeatable environment.

From fake bank portals and telecom billing systems to news sites and government portals — EVWA brings challenges that feel real and relevant to Ethiopian contexts.

Platform Features

Everything a security
researcher needs.

EVWA is not just a static lab — it's a living, evolving cybersecurity training ecosystem with professional-grade tooling built for the next generation of Ethiopian security professionals.

🐳
Docker Isolation
Each challenge runs in its own isolated container. No cross-contamination. Spin up, attack, reset — clean environment every time. Full Docker-based architecture for safe practice.
🏆
CTF Engine
Live CTF competition engine with real-time scoreboard, time-limited challenges, and flag submission system. Host your own team competitions or join public events.
🇪🇹
Amharic Support
Full Amharic language interface and challenge descriptions. Culturally relevant scenarios — Ethiopian bank portals, telecom billing, news sites, and government systems.
📊
Pentest Reports
Auto-generated penetration testing reports after completing challenges. Export professional-grade reports in PDF/HTML format — build your portfolio as you learn.
🏅
Badge System
Earn badges for completing challenges, discovering bugs, and reaching milestones. Showcase your expertise with a verifiable achievement profile visible to employers.
📡
Real-time Scoreboard
WebSocket-powered live scoreboard during CTF events. Watch scores update in real time, see who just captured a flag, and track your team's ranking live.
Difficulty Levels

Four levels. One journey.

LOW
ቀላል

No security controls. Ideal for beginners learning how vulnerabilities work. Direct, clear paths to exploitation with educational explanations.

MEDIUM
መካከለኛ

Basic defenses in place. Input validation, simple filters. Requires understanding of bypass techniques. Closer to real-world junior deployments.

HIGH
ከባድ

Strong security measures. Multiple layers of defense. Requires chaining techniques and creative thinking. Simulates hardened production environments.

IMPOSSIBLE
የማይቻል

Theoretically unexploitable. Demonstrates best security practices. Understand what a truly secure implementation looks like and why it works.

AI Engine

Intelligence that
adapts to you.

EVWA integrates Artificial Intelligence to transform the learning experience from passive instruction to active, personalized mentorship.

🤖 AI Hint System
Analyzes your failed attempts and delivers personalized, context-aware hints without giving away the answer. Guides your thinking, not your solution.
📚 Amharic AI Tutor
Explains vulnerability concepts in Amharic. Ask questions in your native language, get clear answers about SQL injection, XSS, and more.
🧭 Skill Gap Recommender
Analyzes your performance profile and recommends the next challenge to maximize learning velocity based on identified skill gaps.
⚡ Challenge Generator
Auto-generates new challenge scenarios to keep content fresh. Never runs out of practice material — new labs are synthesized on demand.
🛡️ Adaptive Defense Simulation
Simulates intelligent defensive systems that adapt to attacker techniques in real time. The system learns your patterns and evolves its defenses accordingly.
